SEO Optimization in HTML

Introduction to SEO

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of enhancing a website to improve its visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs). Proper SEO can lead to increased traffic and better user engagement.

Importance of Title and Meta Tags

Title and meta tags play a crucial role in SEO. The title tag appears in search engine results and browser tabs, while the meta description provides a brief summary of the page's content, enticing users to click on the link.

Best Practices for Title and Meta Tags

  • Title Tag: Keep it under 60 characters, make it descriptive, and include relevant keywords.
  • Meta Description: Keep it under 160 characters, ensure it is concise and compelling, and include a call to action if possible.
  • Keywords: Use relevant keywords in both the title and meta description, but avoid keyword stuffing.
